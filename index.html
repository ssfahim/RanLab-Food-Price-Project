Food Prices in Newfoundland and Labrador

Explore how prices of common grocery items have changed over time!

Consumers all across Canada have experienced an increase in the prices of common food items. On average, food prices have increased by 3.9% between January 2023 and January 2024. During the same time, food prices in Newfoundland and Labrador have increased by 5.1%.

To better understand the changing prices of food items in Newfoundland and Labrador, we’ve created an interactive tool that displays the prices of common retail food items.

The data used in this tool is from gathered from Statistics Canada’s Consumer Price Index (CPI) which measures changes in prices. Statistics Canada collects scanner data from retailers across Canada to provide the most accurate information possible.

How it Works.

Select the food items that you would like to learn about through data visualization. Once you have made your selection a table and line chart will appear about your selected items.
